EduPing

Summarize announcements and ping them on WhatsApp

Turn raw messages into concise, actionable summaries and distribute them to your contact list in a few clicks.

Launch UI (run: streamlit run eduping_frontend.py)

Upload CSV

Bring your announcements in a CSV with a messages column. Optional phone column per row.

AI Summaries

Backed by Transformers (BART + NER) to extract dates, times, actions, and key entities.

WhatsApp Delivery

Send combined summaries to multiple numbers via WhatsApp Web automation.

Export

Download a summarized CSV for record keeping or sharing.

Quickstart

  1. Install: pip install -r requirements.txt
  2. Run UI: streamlit run eduping_frontend.py
  3. Upload CSV, generate summaries, scan WhatsApp Web, and send.
